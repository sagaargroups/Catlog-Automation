Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 01-09-2025

Introduction

Catalog Automation by cloudfetch.tech ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information about you when you use our Google Sheets add-on.

By using our service, you consent to the data practices described in this policy. If you do not agree with the data practices described, you should not use our add-on.

Data Collection

We only access data necessary to provide our services and improve user experience.

Data Usage

Your data is used solely for the purpose of providing catalog automation services.

Data Protection

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information.

Information We Collect

Our add-on only accesses your Google Sheets data when explicitly activated by you. We collect minimal information necessary to provide our services, including:

  • Sheet Data: We access the content of your Google Sheets to provide catalog automation services. This includes product information, headers, and data structure.
  • Configuration Data: We store your container configurations, rule settings, and automation preferences to maintain your setup between sessions.
  • Usage Data: We collect basic usage statistics to improve our service, including frequency of use, features accessed, and performance metrics.
  • Technical Information: We may collect information about your browser type, operating system, and device type for compatibility purposes.

We do not collect personally identifiable information beyond what is necessary to provide our services.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Process and organize your catalog data according to your rules and container configurations
  • Provide and maintain our service, including saving your automation settings between sessions
  • Improve and optimize our add-on based on usage patterns and performance metrics
  • Communicate with you about updates, new features, and important service changes
  • Ensure the security and integrity of our service
  • Troubleshoot technical issues and provide customer support

We do not use your data for advertising purposes or sell your information to third parties.

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personally identifiable information to outside parties. This does not include trusted third parties who assist us in operating our website, conducting our business, or servicing you, so long as those parties agree to keep this information confidential.

We may share information in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We may share information with third-party vendors who provide services on our behalf, such as hosting, analytics, and customer support. These vendors are contractually obligated to protect your data.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or a portion of our assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

We require all third parties to respect the security of your personal data and to treat it in accordance with the law.

Data Security

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information when you use our add-on. All data is encrypted and stored securely following industry best practices.

Our security measures include:

  • Encryption of data both in transit and at rest
  • Regular security assessments and vulnerability testing
  • Access controls limiting who can access user data
  • Secure development practices and regular code reviews

While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but we work diligently to protect your information.

Data Retention

We retain your information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which we collected it, including to provide our services and comply with legal obligations.

Your container configurations and rule settings are stored until you choose to delete them or until you uninstall our add-on. Usage data is typically retained for 12 months to help us improve our services.

You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at privacy@cloudfetch.tech.

Your Data Protection Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: You have the right to request copies of your personal data.
  • Rectification: You have the right to request that we correct any information you believe is inaccurate.
  • Erasure: You have the right to request that we erase your personal data, under certain conditions.
  • Restriction: You have the right to request that we restrict the processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.
  • Data Portability: You have the right to request that we transfer the data that we have collected to another organization, or directly to you, under certain conditions.

If you make a request, we have one month to respond to you. If you would like to exercise any of these rights, please contact us at privacy@cloudfetch.tech.

Children's Privacy

Our service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us. If we become aware that we have collected personal data from children without verification of parental consent, we take steps to remove that information from our servers.

Changes to Our Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this Privacy Policy.

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
